Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority — read our complete policy

Advertisement
📅 Last Updated: January 15, 2026

At AI ToolCor Calculators ("we", "us", "our"), accessible at calculator.aitoolcor.com, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

🔒 Quick Summary: All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store your input data. We only collect anonymous analytics and serve ads through Google AdSense.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our calculators, all data you enter (amounts, dates, percentages, etc.) is processed entirely in your browser. We do NOT store, save, or transmit this data to our servers.

When you contact us through the contact form, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Subject of inquiry
  • Message content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Country/region (general location)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and popular calculators
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use:

3.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly (theme preferences, form data).

3.2 Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense cookies to serve relevant ads. These may track your interests based on your browsing.

🍪 Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may affect website functionality.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • About Ads (US)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

Read Google's complete privacy policy at: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — For displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics — For website analytics
  • Google Fonts — For web typography
  • Font Awesome (CDN) — For icons
  • Cloudflare CDN — For faster content delivery

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No server-side storage of calculator inputs
  • Limited access to contact form data
  • Regular security updates to website code
✅ Important: Since calculations happen in your browser, your financial/health data NEVER reaches our servers!

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are a resident of EU, UK, or California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — Request what data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability — Get your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object — Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Opt-Out — Opt out of targeted advertising

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@aitoolcor.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

  • Calculator inputs: Never stored (browser-side only)
  • Contact form data: Retained for 1 year for support purposes
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months (Google default)
  • Server logs: Retained for 30 days for security

10. International Data Transfers

Our servers may be located outside your country. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Major changes will be highlighted on our homepage.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • 📧 Email: privacy@aitoolcor.com
  • 📧 General: support@aitoolcor.com
  • 🌐 Website: calculator.aitoolcor.com
  • 📝 Contact Form: Contact Page
📋 Summary: We respect your privacy, don't store calculator data, use cookies for analytics & ads, and comply with GDPR/CCPA. For any questions, contact us anytime.
