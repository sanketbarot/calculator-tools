Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority — read our complete policy

Advertisement
📅 Last Updated: January 15, 2026

At AI ToolCor Calculators ("we", "us", "our"), accessible at calculator.aitoolcor.com, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

🔒 Quick Summary: All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store your input data. We only collect anonymous analytics and serve ads through Google AdSense.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our calculators, all data you enter (amounts, dates, percentages) is processed entirely in your browser. We do NOT store, save, or transmit this data to our servers.

When you contact us, we may collect: name, email, subject, and message content.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Country/region (general location)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

2. How We Use Information

  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Tracking

3.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics cookies to understand visitor behavior.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense cookies to serve relevant ads.

🍪 Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may affect website functionality.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits.

You may opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • About Ads (US)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

Read Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense — Advertisements
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics
  • Google Fonts — Web typography
  • Font Awesome (CDN) — Icons
  • Cloudflare CDN — Content delivery

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No server-side storage of calculator inputs
  • Limited access to contact form data
  • Regular security updates
✅ Important: Since calculations happen in your browser, your financial/health data NEVER reaches our servers!

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

EU, UK, or California residents have:

  • Right to Access — Request your data
  • Right to Rectification — Correct data
  • Right to Erasure — Delete data
  • Right to Restrict Processing
  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to Object
  • Right to Opt-Out of targeted ads

Email us at privacy@aitoolcor.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Retention

  • Calculator inputs: Never stored
  • Contact form data: 1 year
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google default)
  • Server logs: 30 days

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

  • 📧 Email: privacy@aitoolcor.com
  • 📧 General: support@aitoolcor.com
  • 🌐 Website: calculator.aitoolcor.com
  • 📝 Contact Form: Contact Page
📋 Summary: We respect your privacy, don't store calculator data, use cookies for analytics & ads, and comply with GDPR/CCPA.
