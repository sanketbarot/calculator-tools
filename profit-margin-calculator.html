About Profit Margin Calculator

Whether you're running an e-commerce store, a retail shop, a manufacturing business, or providing services, knowing your profit margin is critical for success. Our Profit Margin Calculator helps you calculate profit, margin %, markup %, and selling price using 4 different calculation modes.

📐 Key Formulas

1. Profit Calculation:

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price

2. Profit Margin (based on Selling Price):

Margin % = (Profit / Selling Price) × 100

3. Markup (based on Cost Price):

Markup % = (Profit / Cost Price) × 100

4. Selling Price from Cost & Margin:

Selling Price = Cost / (1 − Margin/100)

5. Selling Price from Cost & Markup:

Selling Price = Cost × (1 + Markup/100)

🎯 What is Profit Margin?

Profit Margin is the percentage of revenue that is profit. It tells you how much of each rupee earned becomes profit after subtracting costs. It's one of the most important metrics for business health.

⚖️ Margin vs Markup: The Crucial Difference

Many people confuse Margin and Markup, but they are different:

Aspect Margin Markup
Based OnSelling PriceCost Price
FormulaProfit/Selling × 100Profit/Cost × 100
AlwaysLess than 100%Can be > 100%
Used ByAccountants, FinanceRetailers, Buyers

Example: Cost ₹100, Sell ₹150, Profit ₹50

  • Margin = 50/150 × 100 = 33.33%
  • Markup = 50/100 × 100 = 50%

💡 Common Markup Examples

  • Wholesale to Retail: 100% markup (₹100 wholesale → ₹200 retail)
  • Jewelry: 200-400% markup
  • Restaurant Food: 200-300% markup
  • Bottled Water: 4,000% markup
  • Designer Clothes: 300-700% markup
  • Movie Theater Popcorn: 1,000-1,300% markup

🎯 Tips for Healthy Profit Margins

  • Know your costs: Include all hidden costs (shipping, packaging, fees)
  • Research competitors: Price competitively but profitably
  • Add value: Better product/service → higher margin tolerance
  • Bulk purchasing: Lower cost = higher margin
  • Reduce overhead: Cut unnecessary expenses
  • Increase prices gradually: Test customer reaction
  • Bundle products: Higher perceived value
  • Focus on high-margin items: Promote them more

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Confusing margin with markup
  • Not including all costs (forgot to add GST, shipping, etc.)
  • Pricing too low to compete (race to the bottom)
  • Ignoring fixed costs in calculations
  • Not adjusting prices for inflation
  • Forgetting payment gateway/platform fees
  • Underestimating returns/refund impact
