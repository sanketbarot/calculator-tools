Frequently Asked Questions

1

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. The formula is: BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)². It helps categorize if a person is underweight, normal, overweight or obese.

2

What is a healthy BMI range?

A healthy BMI range is 18.5 to 24.9. Below 18.5 is underweight, 25-29.9 is overweight, and 30+ is considered obese.

3

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a good general indicator but may not be accurate for athletes with high muscle mass, elderly people, pregnant women, or children. Consult a doctor for a complete health assessment.

4

What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs at rest to maintain basic life functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production. It varies by age, gender, weight and height.

5

Is my data stored anywhere?

No! All calculations run in your browser. Your health data is never sent to any server. 100% private and secure.
