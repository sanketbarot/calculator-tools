Frequently Asked Questions

1

How accurate is this calculator?

Accurate to the second, accounting for leap years and varying month lengths. The live seconds counter updates in real-time.

2

Can I calculate age at a specific date?

Yes! Fill in the "Age at Date" field to calculate your age at any past or future date instead of today.

3

Does it handle leap years?

Yes, all calculations properly handle leap years including February 29 birthdays.

4

Is my data stored anywhere?

No! Everything runs entirely in your browser. Your date of birth is never sent to any server. 100% private and secure.

5

What is the zodiac sign based on?

Western tropical astrology is used for zodiac signs. Chinese zodiac follows the traditional 12-year animal cycle based on birth year.
