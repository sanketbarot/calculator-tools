About Age Calculator

Our Age Calculator gives you your exact age in multiple formats — years, months, days, hours, minutes, and even seconds. Plus, get your zodiac sign, birthstone, day of week you were born, and countdown to your next birthday — all in one place!

📐 How Age is Calculated

Age = Current Date − Date of Birth

The calculation considers:

  • Years — Full years between dates
  • Months — Remaining months after years
  • Days — Remaining days after months
  • Leap years (29 days in February every 4 years)
  • Variable month lengths (28-31 days)

✅ How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your Date of Birth using the date picker
  2. Select Calculate Age At (default: today)
  3. Use Today button for current age
  4. Use Next Birthday button to see age on next birthday
  5. Click Calculate to view full age breakdown
  6. See years, months, days, hours, minutes, seconds
  7. Discover your zodiac sign and birthstone
