About SIP Calculator

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) Calculator helps you estimate the future value of your monthly mutual fund investments. Whether you're a beginner or experienced investor, this tool helps you plan your wealth-building journey effectively.

📐 SIP Formula

FV = P × ({[(1 + r)^n - 1] / r}) × (1 + r)

Where:

  • FV = Future Value (maturity amount)
  • P = Monthly investment amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate (Annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • n = Number of months (Years × 12)

✅ How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose between SIP (Monthly) or Lumpsum investment
  2. Enter your Monthly Investment Amount
  3. Enter the Expected Annual Return Rate (typically 12-15% for equity)
  4. Enter the Investment Period in years
  5. See your future value, total invested & wealth gained instantly

💡 Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Rupee Cost Averaging — buy more units when prices are low
  • Power of Compounding — earn returns on returns
  • Disciplined Investing — auto-debit ensures regular savings
  • Affordable — start with as little as ₹500/month
  • Flexible — increase, pause or stop anytime

📊 Example Calculation

If you invest ₹5,000/month for 10 years at 12% expected return:

  • Total Invested: ₹6,00,000
  • Future Value: ₹11,61,695
  • Wealth Gained: ₹5,61,695

🎯 SIP vs Lumpsum: Which is Better?

SIP is better for salaried individuals as it spreads risk over time and doesn't require large capital upfront. Lumpsum works well when markets are low or you have a large amount to invest. Try both options in this calculator!
