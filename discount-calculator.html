About Discount Calculator

Shopping made smarter! Our Discount Calculator helps you quickly calculate the actual price you pay after any discount. It supports single discounts (Flat 50% Off), double discounts (50% + Extra 10%), triple discounts (Flipkart Big Sale), and even adds GST after discount.

📐 Discount Formula

Single Discount:

Discount Amount = Original Price × (Discount % / 100)
Sale Price = Original Price − Discount Amount

Multiple Discounts (Successive):

After D1: Price × (1 − D1/100)
After D2: Price × (1 − D1/100) × (1 − D2/100)
After D3: Price × (1 − D1/100) × (1 − D2/100) × (1 − D3/100)

🛍️ Common Shopping Scenarios

  • Flat 50% Off — Single discount, simple savings
  • Buy 1 Get 1 Free — Equivalent to 50% off
  • Flat 40% + Extra 10% Off — Double discount (effective ~46%)
  • Up to 70% Off + 10% Coupon — Double discount
  • Sale + Coupon + Bank Offer — Triple discount

⚠️ Multiple Discounts vs Flat Discount

Are "30% + 20% Off" same as "50% Off"? NO!

Let's see for ₹1000:

  • Flat 50%: ₹1000 − ₹500 = ₹500 (You save ₹500)
  • 30% + 20%: ₹1000 × 0.7 × 0.8 = ₹560 (You save ₹440)

Flat 50% is better by ₹60! Always calculate effective discount.

✅ How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select Discount Type — Single, Double, or Triple
  2. Enter Original Price (MRP)
  3. Enter Discount % for each level
  4. Optional: Tick GST to add tax after discount
  5. See Final Price, Savings, and Effective Discount

💡 Pro Shopping Tips

  • Always check final price — not just discount %
  • Compare with original MRP — sometimes inflated
  • Use coupons — extra savings on top of sales
  • Bank offers — get instant cashback/discount
  • Wait for big sales — Diwali, Christmas, EOSS
  • Check return policy — sale items may not be returnable
