Frequently Asked Questions

1

How do I calculate percentage of a number?

To find X% of Y, multiply Y by X and divide by 100. For example, 25% of 200 = (200 × 25) / 100 = 50.

2

How do I calculate percentage change?

Percentage change = ((New - Old) / Old) × 100. For example, from 80 to 100: ((100-80)/80) × 100 = 25% increase.

3

What is percentage difference?

Percentage difference = (|A - B| / ((A + B) / 2)) × 100. It measures the relative difference between two values compared to their average.

4

How to increase a number by percentage?

To increase a number by X%, multiply it by (1 + X/100). For example, 200 increased by 15% = 200 × 1.15 = 230.

5

Is my data stored?

No! All calculations run in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Calculation history is stored only in your current session and is cleared when you close the page.
