Frequently Asked Questions

A Loan Calculator is a tool to estimate your monthly EMI, total interest payable, and total repayment amount for any type of loan based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

An amortization schedule is a detailed table showing each EMI payment broken down into principal and interest, along with the remaining loan balance after each payment. Helps track loan progress.

You can reduce EMI by increasing loan tenure, making higher down payment, choosing lower interest rate, or refinancing your existing loan. Each option has trade-offs to consider.

Home loans typically have the lowest interest rates (8-9%) because they are secured against property. Personal loans have the highest (11-16%) as they are unsecured.

Yes, prepayment reduces your outstanding principal, which means less interest in future EMIs. It's especially beneficial in early years when most EMI goes to interest. Check for prepayment charges.

Yes! Longer tenure = Lower EMI but Higher total interest. Example: ₹50L @ 8.5% for 15 years pays ₹38.7L interest, while 25 years pays ₹70L interest. Choose tenure wisely!

A credit score of 750+ is ideal for getting loans at the best interest rates. Scores between 700-750 may still get approved but at higher rates. Below 700 may face rejection or very high rates.
